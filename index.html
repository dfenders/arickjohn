About Arick

Arick John, who's not a new name to the real estate industry, started his passion for selling residential properties in 2011 with Ray White in the metropolitan jungle of West Auckland. He says this was a great place to learn real estate.

"It’s cutthroat in Auckland and you need to sink or swim in that environment. I also loved the diverse character of the area and its people in Glen Eden."

From Auckland, he then moved to the relaxing but cooler deep South working for Ray White Invercargill for the past four years. In his second year in Invercargill, Arick achieved Premier Member Award and Customer Satisfaction Award - fast becoming known as the Double Award Winning Agent. Arick had a one-year stint with Ray White Dunedin but missed the relaxed culture of Invercargill and has since moved back, rearing to go.

In his over 10 years of real estate career Arick received several awards in recognition of his success as a Salesperson and having sold with the same company throughout his career, Arick believes stability is very important for success.

Arick believes in quality service. “I’m pretty finicky in some things and the service you get from me is the same quality of service I would like to receive myself. It’s about communication, listening to your wants and needs, being honest, making it stress-free, and ensuring I do the job to the best of my ability. Provide these things and results always follow!”

So if you are looking to sell your present home or investment property or you are looking to buy your next home, or you want a FREE no-obligation market appraisal call Arick today.
