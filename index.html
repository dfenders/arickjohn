About Arick

Arick John, not a new name to the real estate industry, started his passion of selling residential properties in 2011 with Ray White in the metropolitan jungle of West Auckland. He says "This was a great place to learn real estate, it’s cut throat in Auckland and you need to sink or swim in that environment. I also loved the diverse character of the area and its people in Glen Eden." From Auckland, he then moved to the relaxing but cooler deep South working for Ray White Invercargill for the past four years. In his second year in Invercargill, Arick achieved Premier Member Award and Customer Satisfaction Award - fast becoming known as the Double Award Winning Agent. In his over 10 years of real estate career Arick received several awards in recognition of his success as a Salesperson and having sold with the same company throughout his career, Arick believes stability is very important for success. “It was a big decision to move to Dunedin but one that we are not regretting. We love being nearer to our children, we love the pace of life and we especially love the diversity of the town. Arick believes in quality service. “I’m pretty finicky in some things and the service you get from me is the same quality of service I would like to receive myself. It’s about communication, listening to your wants and needs, being honest, making it stress free and ensuring I do the job to the best of my ability. Provide these things and results always follow!” So if you are looking to sell your present home or investment property or you are looking to buy your next home, or you want a FREE no-obligation market appraisal call Arick today.
