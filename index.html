About the team

With a shared commitment to excellence and over 20 years of combined experience, Team Farhan and Arick bring together the perfect balance of industry knowledge, strategic insight, and client-focused service.

Arick John has been a trusted name in real estate since 2011. Starting his career with Ray White in West Auckland, Arick quickly built a reputation for reliability, clear communication, and delivering results. His dedication to client satisfaction earned him both the Premier Member Award and the Customer Satisfaction Award during his tenure at Ray White Invercargill. After a period working in Dunedin, Arick returned to Invercargill where he continues to help clients navigate the property market with confidence and ease. Known for his straightforward, no-stress approach, Arick is passionate about making the buying and selling process seamless and successful.

Farhan Malik joins the real estate world with a rich background in engineering, business, and entrepreneurship. Holding a Bachelor’s degree in Civil Engineering and a Postgraduate qualification in Business, Farhan has spent over a decade building his career in Southland. His experience running his own business for five years sharpened his skills in communication, customer care, and relationship building—values he now brings to every real estate interaction. Having personally bought, sold, and invested in property, Farhan understands the emotional and financial journey clients go through and is driven to provide a real estate experience grounded in honesty, transparency, and genuine support.

Together, Farhan and Arick form a dynamic, approachable, and knowledgeable team. Their complementary strengths—Arick’s real estate expertise and negotiation skills, paired with Farhan’s analytical thinking and client-first approach—mean clients receive well-rounded service with a personal touch.

Whether you're buying your first home, investing, or selling a property, Team Farhan and Arick are here to make the journey clear, stress-free, and rewarding. Reach out today and experience the difference a dedicated team can make.
